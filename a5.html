Plexiglas work - Friendship(?)


Materials: Acrylic, metallic sharpies, black sharpie, paper on Plexiglas

This illustration on Plexiglas has double sides of view. The theme I am portraying through this artwork is the betrayal of friendship, and the story told here has two perspectives. On the front, there is the stage with a mask that covers two puppets holding hand to show they are friends. While at the back, the whole bodies of the puppets are revealed, and there are holes on the body of each puppet to show these "friends" have been hurting each other under self-reasons.
