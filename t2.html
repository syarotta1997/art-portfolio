DMCI CyberARTS Brochure design


Program: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign

In this project, my goal was to design the CyberARTS program open house brochure with an assigned style. My style is student's artwork, and I've decided to Photoshop these artworks and compose them together as a new piece. For the color scheme, I chose the violet series because it is similar to red, which is CyberARTS's color. As for the working tool, all designs are being made in Adobe Photoshop and then imported into Adobe InDesign for layout designs.
