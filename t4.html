Game Concept design - After Rebirth


Program: Adobe Photoshop, EasyPaintTool SAI, Adobe InDesign

The concept art for Role Playing Gaming "After Rebirth" is one of my summative projects. In this work, I've designed a general concept for a game, which involves two major character design, armor and clothing design, monster design and landscape design. The background story tells that the world has been through a period of chaos and a great magician has resolved the conflict by destroying everything as a reform. After a few years of new peaceful world, the great magician returns and decides to reform the world again, in order to avoid the corruption. The object for the main characters (player) is to investigate this issue through a journey and resolve this conflict.
