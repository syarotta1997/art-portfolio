About

Graduate of CyberARTS specialized program
          Helen has a strong interest in the Arts and Design field and she has been studying fine arts since 2009. In 2011 Helen joined the CyberARTS specialized program in Don Mills Collegiate Institute and developed further skills in Graphic Design. As a designer, Helen has the experience in using the Abode series software including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, DreamWeaver and Soundbooth. She is also capable in doing digital illustration using EasyPaintTool SAI. As an artist, Helen has the strength in making fine arts using different materials and techniques involving those shown in this portfolio. She is able to create an art that is imaginative, that tells a story, and that holds a meaning in her life. As future career, Helen is looking forward to continue her study in the game design field.
